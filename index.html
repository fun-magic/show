About Me

Easy Instructions Just for Kids!

I help young kids, usually between the ages of 4 and 7, learn how to use their magic kits when they receive them as gifts for birthdays, holidays, or other special occasions. Often, they’re excited but don’t understand how the tricks work, so I step in to show them. I guide them through each step, explain how the props are used, and help them practice until they can perform the tricks on their own. I really enjoy helping because I want to inspire a new generation of magicians and share the joy and wonder that magic can bring.

Magic Shows

I perform magic shows for kids at parties and public events, using a mix of stage and close-up magic to keep them engaged and amazed. For adults, I focus more on card tricks and close-up illusions that create a sense of mystery and surprise. I love involving the audience—whether it’s having a child help with a trick or showing someone magic right in front of them, their reactions are always the best part. What I enjoy most is how magic can fool people just enough to make them feel like real magic exists in the world.
